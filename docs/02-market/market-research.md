# Market Research

## Overview
The global demand for data professionals has increased significantly over the past decade. Organizations rely on data-driven decision-making, creating demand for skilled data analysts, data scientists, and ML engineers.

## Market Size

The data analytics market is projected to grow rapidly due to:

- Increased enterprise data generation
- Adoption of AI-driven decision systems
- Expansion of cloud-based data infrastructure

## Growth Drivers

### 1. Data-driven businesses
Companies rely heavily on analytics for decision making.

### 2. AI adoption
AI and machine learning require large volumes of processed data.

### 3. Data talent shortage
Companies struggle to find skilled analysts and data scientists.
